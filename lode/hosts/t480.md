# t480

Primary laptop. ThinkPad T480, x86_64.

## Location

`hosts/t480/`:
- `default.nix`, `configuration.nix`, `hardware-configuration.nix`, `disko-config.nix`, `secrets.nix`
- `services/`: `default.nix`, `desktop/` (xdg), `mixxx.nix`, `teleport.nix`, `wireguard.nix`

## Role

Daily-driver laptop with Hyprland desktop (composed via home-manager features), WireGuard VPN, Mixxx DJ software, and Teleport.

## Wiring in flake

`nixosConfigurations.t480` + `homeConfigurations."rocksus@t480"` (modules: disko, agenix).
